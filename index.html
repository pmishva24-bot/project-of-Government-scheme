?? About the Hub

The Indian Government Schemes Hub is a non-official initiative designed to simplify access to Central Government welfare programs. We aggregate information from various official portals to provide a centralized search experience.

Our Goals:

  • To break down bureaucratic language into simple terms.
  • To provide direct links to the official government application portals.
  • To remain transparent about our data sources.

? Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I apply for a scheme?

A: We provide direct official links. You must visit the respective ministry's website (linked in the card details) and follow their specific online application procedure. We do not handle applications directly.

Q: Is my state eligible for these schemes?

A: Most schemes listed are Central Government schemes applicable across India. However, some may require state government consent or specific eligibility criteria. Always check the official website for state-specific rules.

Q: What documents are usually needed?

A: Typically, Aadhaar card, proof of income, bank account details (for direct benefit transfer), and residence proof are mandatory for most welfare schemes.
